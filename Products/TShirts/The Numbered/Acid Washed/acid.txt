Fabric
Crafted from 100% Super Combed Cotton with a 240 GSM heavyweight construction. Pre-shrunk, bio-washed, and finished with a unique acid wash treatment for a soft, lived-in feel and distinctive vintage character.

Fit
Unisex oversized fit with dropped shoulders and a relaxed streetwear silhouette.

Features
Every garment features a unique acid-wash finish, making no two pieces exactly alike. Durable double-stitched construction and a Lycra ribbed neck ensure lasting comfort and shape retention.

Care
Machine wash cold, inside out with similar colours. Hang dry or tumble dry on low. Iron inside out on low heat. Do not bleach or iron directly on the print
